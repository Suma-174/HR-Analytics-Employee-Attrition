# HR Analytics: Employee Attrition Dashboard

## Overview
The **Employee Attrition Dashboard** is an HR analytics project designed to help organizations **monitor, analyze, and reduce employee turnover**. By visualizing workforce trends, HR teams can **identify high-risk groups, understand attrition patterns, and implement proactive retention strategies**.

This dashboard is built using **Power BI** with measures, KPIs, and interactive visualizations to provide actionable insights into employee attrition.

---

## Key Objectives
- Track overall **employee attrition** and retention trends.
- Analyze **attrition by department, role, tenure, and location**.
- Identify **patterns and risk factors** contributing to turnover.
- Support HR strategies for **employee engagement and workforce planning**.

---

## Key Metrics & KPIs
- **Total Employees** – Current number of employees in the organization.
- **Attrition Count** – Number of employees leaving during a period.
- **Attrition Rate** – Percentage of employees leaving relative to total workforce.
- **Average Tenure** – Average duration employees stay with the company.
- **Department-wise & Role-wise Attrition** – Identify high-risk areas.
- **Gender & Location Distribution** – Diversity and location-based insights.

---

## Visual Components
- **KPI Cards** – Total Employees, Attrition Count, Attrition Rate, Average Tenure.
- **Bar/Column Charts** – Attrition by Department, Role, and Job Level.
- **Line Charts** – Monthly or yearly attrition trends over time.
- **Pie/Donut Charts** – Gender and location distribution of employees leaving.
- **Tables** – Detailed employee-level data for deep-dive analysis.

---

## Benefits
- Early detection of high-risk areas to prevent turnover.
- Supports **data-driven HR decisions** on hiring, training, and retention.
- Provides a **snapshot of workforce health** for management reviews.
- Enhances **employee engagement initiatives** by identifying potential issues.

---

## Tools Used
- **Power BI** – For building interactive dashboards and KPIs.
- **Excel / CSV** – Data preprocessing and cleaning.
- **DAX** – For creating measures, KPIs, and calculations.


---

## How to Use
1. Download the dataset (CSV/Excel) from the repository or your own HR data.
2. Open the Power BI Desktop file (`.pbix`) included in this repository.
3. Interact with the dashboard by clicking on KPIs, charts, or slicers to filter insights.
4. Customize metrics or visuals based on your organizational requirements.

---

## Conclusion
This dashboard provides **HR managers and executives** with actionable insights into employee attrition. By monitoring workforce trends and identifying high-risk areas, organizations can **improve retention, enhance engagement, and make informed strategic decisions**.
